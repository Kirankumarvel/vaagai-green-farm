Quick Answers

Is Vaagai Green Farm completely natural?
Yes – we never use synthetic pesticides or chemicals. Our farming is 100% natural since 2020.
Do you deliver fresh vegetables?
We harvest every morning and deliver within 24 hours to families around Arakkonam.
Can I visit the farm or join a workshop?
Of course! We run community workshops on hydroponics, beekeeping, and natural growing. Reach out to schedule a visit.
