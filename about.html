Cultivating Change Since 2020

Vaagai Green Farm isn't just a farm — it's a movement toward sustainable, organic, and accessible food for urban India. Based in Arakkonam, Tamil Nadu, we blend ancient farming wisdom with cutting-edge agritech.

🎯

Our Mission

Democratizing healthy food by making pesticide-free, organic vegetables and sustainable farming knowledge affordable & available to every urban household.

👁️

Our Vision

A carbon-neutral India where every city embraces rooftop & community farming, reducing food miles and nourishing both people and the planet.

💚

Core Values

Zero-waste, regenerative agriculture, community empowerment, transparency, and innovation for long-term ecological balance.

🌟 The Vaagai Story: What started as a small family farm has grown into a thriving agritech hub. We saw the gap between conventional chemical farming and genuine organic produce. Today, we operate hydroponic towers, aquaponic systems, and bee colonies — all while training local youth in sustainable agriculture.

Our commitment: every vegetable we grow is 100% chemical-free, harvested within hours of delivery, and cultivated with water-saving technology. Join us in reimagining the future of food.
